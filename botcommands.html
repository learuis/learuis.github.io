Teleports

Return Home - v/home

Teleports you to your bed/bedroll. Consumes 2 Bronze Coins.

v/home, v/homelist, v/home1, v/home2, v/home3, v/home4
Use v/homelist to see your list of spawn points. Spawn points are organized by x-coordinate, meaning that your Exiled Lands spawn points will always be lower in the list than your Siptah spawn points. You can have 4 possible targets for this command: One bed and One Bedroll in each of the Exiled Lands and the Isle of Siptah.

Boon Information - v/booninfo

Reports on current boon status.

v/booninfo, v/boon, v/boons, v/binfo
This command shows information about the Community Boons system. It explains which Boons are active, how long they are active for, and what item needs to be turned in to add more time. It also shows the current experience multipliers for the profession system.
