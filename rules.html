1 - COMMUNITY RULES

The rules below keep our community a safe and healthy place for all of our members. Please read them carefully. You are responsible as a player for knowing and understanding these rules. If there is something you do not understand, please ask a moderator. Saying you were not aware of the rules is not excusable if you violate them.

(1.1) AGE 🔞

As Conan is an 18+ rated game, it is expected that you are at least 18, and your character is at least 18. Underage players and role-playing as an underage character are not permitted.

(1.2) RESPECT 🤝

Be respectful of other players. If you have an issue with another player, it is your responsibility to be an adult and talk to them about it. If you cannot find resolution, then you can choose to not interact with that player. The moderation team is not here to babysit.

(1.3) THIRST 🥵

Do not be thirsty, creepy, make inappropriate comments to other members, or DM them without permission. If someone is not responding to your advances, do not persist and make them uncomfortable.

(1.4) ERP 🍆

We are a community of adults and Erotic Roleplay (ERP) between consenting members is permitted. If you engage in ERP please do so privately.

(1.5) HATE / BULLYING 🤬

Hate speech, racism, sexism, bigotry, racial slurs, and bullying are not permitted and will result in an immediate ban. If your profile, username, or messages contain anything of the above you will be removed.

(1.6) TROLLS 👹

Trolls will be banned and sacrificed to the blood god. The reality of playing in a shared online environment is that we have a collective responsibility to make the server an enjoyable experience for each other. The following rules are intended to ensure an equitable experience for all players while reducing server lag.

2 - BUILDING RULES

(2.1) BUILDING LOCATION 🗺️

You can build anywhere on the map that the game allows but do not build close to other players without permission. Get permission from the owner of the existing base or look for somewhere else to build. This can be checked with a repeatable process: to check distance, stand at the closest edge of the base, set "View Distance" (Under Settings -> Video) to HIGH. Log out and back in. If the other base loads in without you moving, it is considered to be "within render distance". If you are unsure, please contact a Moderator.

(2.2) BUILDING LIMITS 🏡

Each solo player / clan may have:
  • One main base per member
  • One auxiliary base (10x10) TOTAL
  • Three outposts (5x5) TOTAL
  • Total Building Pieces x4000 per member
  • Total Placeable Items x1200 per member

You can check counts by looking at the clan tab.

Examples:

  • 2 person clan = 8000 pieces, 2400 items, 2 main bases, 1 aux base, 3 outposts.
  • 3 person clan = 12000 pieces, 3600 items, 3 main bases, 1 aux base, 3 outposts.

(2.3) OUTPOSTS ⛺️

You may build three (3) outposts. Outposts have the following limits:

  • No larger than a 5x5 footprint, top to bottom
  • No taller than 6 blocks in height
  • Any stairs / entrance must fit inside the 5x5 footprint
  • No outposts within render distance of another that you own

Moderators reserve the right to make a ruling on excessive outposts for the best interest of the server.

Outposts do not claim render distance like bases, but must respect the render distance existing player bases. Outposts can be built next to outposts owned by other players. Construction of a new main base by a player does not force existing outposts to move.

(2.4) STRAY OBJECTS

Placeable objects that are not near your base or aux base are considered to be Outposts. A thrall wheel, animal pen, or TP stone placed on the ground is an Outpost and counts toward your limits, even if there are no foundations.

  • Bedrolls are an exception and are not Outposts.
  • Spamming foundations / pillars to claim land is not allowed.

(2.5) BLOCKING ⛔

Do not "wall in" resources or block access to them. Do not block the spawn locations of world bosses, animal cubs, or horses. Do not block throughways, paths, or cave entrances. If you build on an important throughway you must provide passage through or around your base.

(2.6) BUILDING LAG MANAGEMENT🧱

Please read the Building Tips forum post for helpful guides and images to demonstrate the best practices for building.

The server is a shared space and excessively large / laggy bases are problematic for the entire community. Be proactive and consider performance around your base. Bases that cause significant drops in FPS for other players may need to be altered. While measurement tools for this are limited, the moderator team reserves the right to make rulings based on the best interests of the entire server.

(2.7) PUT EFFORT INTO YOUR BUILDS

Help make the server look nice! Put some effort into your buildings and be creative. Do not build giant sandstone cubes. If you struggle with building, there are plenty of guides and tips in discord server to help get you started. There are also many helpful players who will be happy to give you some tips.

(2.8) NEWB RIVER RENDER DISTANCE EXEMPTION

To allow space for new players to build starter bases in, we have created an exemption to the Render Distance rule for the following map squares: E3, E4, F3, F4, G3, G4, H3, H4, I3, I4, J3, J4. Base built in these squares do not claim "render distance" around them. Please try to be courteous with your placement, but you don't need to go out of render distance to set up your starter base.

(2.9) MAJOR CITY RENDER DISTANCE EXEMPTION

The Render Distance rule will not be enforced in a small radius around major NPC cities, including Summoning Place, The Den, The Mounds, Sepermeru, Buccaneer Bay, and New Asagarth. A map with the boundaries is available on discord. This does not mean that you should build as close to possible to your neighbors, please be courteous.

(2.10) OUTPOSTS ONLY AT SINKHOLE

The "valley" where the Sinkhole located is a designated space for Outposts only (5x5 size!). It is intended to be a central hub for player Market and Shop builds.

3 - NO STEALING

Conan Exiles is a game of inherent risk and loss, but this server is PVE. Theft is only permitted in roleplaying situations with consent from all involved parties.

(3.1) PROTECT YOURSELF

Although stealing is not allowed, new players may join the server and not check the rules. Such situations will be dealt with, but to avoid losing play time, place your valuables in locked chests or inside enclosed buildings.

It is important to know that wheels and workbenches cannot be locked and are inherently at risk. Containers / benches can also be accessed through windows so be mindful of placement.

All cases of theft that are reported will be investigated and items can be fully replaced. However, because this takes staff time and effort to resolve, please do your best to lock up your things to minimize the chance of this happening.

(3.2) DECAYED BASES

If you come across a base that shows "Decayed" when checked with a repair hammer, you have permission to demolish that base and take anything you want from inside.

(3.3) CLEAN UP

Do not break the door on a decayed base, cherry pick loot, and move on. Please demolish it completely when you are finished. This helps keep the server clean, refreshes building spots faster than natural decay, avoids wasted time for players and mods, and prevents decayed looting from being confused with theft. If you can't demolish the build for some reason, notify a moderator.

4 - ROLEPLAYING & CHARACTER NAMES

While RP is not mandatory on this server, we do try to create an environment where immersive roleplaying can happen for players who enjoy that kind of play. The following rules are intended to support that kind of experience.

(4.1) CHARACTER / CLAN NAMES

Your character and clan name must be RP / lore-friendly. That means the names reasonably fit to a fantasy-themed universe, like the one Conan Exiles takes place in. Names do not have to be lore-specific to Conan, they just can't be glaringly off-theme.

Names must be properly formatted, including spaces / capitalization, and without numbers.

Your names must not:

  • Feature racism, sexism, or hate speech
  • Reference real people / celebrities
  • Reference off-theme pop culture characters
  • Reference characters from other fiction or IPs (no Gandalf, Ragnar, etc)

Examples of unacceptable names: Exile6969, Taylor Swift, Ronald McDonald, David Duke, Mickey Mouse, LakersFan, PussyBlaster2000, rulerofhYBoRIA

If your name does not violate the rules above you are free to be creative and select whatever name you want. If you are having trouble, you can try a name generator. Moderators reserve the right to enforce a name change in the best interest of the community.

(4.2) ROLEPLAYING

RP is not mandatory on the server and you do not have to participate if you do not wish. If you wish to engage in RP there are several regular events that provide an opportunity for you to connect with other players who engage in RP.

(4.3) ROLEPLAYING LIMITS

RP that involves underage characters, bestiality, rape, hate speech, racism, genocide, extreme violence / gore, or other themes that violate the servers rules are not permitted.

RP must happen on a scale and tone that is appropriate to the game world.

5 - STAFF COMMITMENT

(5.1) BAND OF OUTCASTS STAFF CODE OF CONDUCT

Our Staff consists of the Admin team, Moderator Team, and Helper Team. This is our commitment to the players of this server.

The role of Staff is to help the community and create a positive environment.

  • Staff will treat players and each other with respect.
  • Staff will record the usage of admin powers in an admin-log.
  • Staff will apply rules equally and fairly to themselves and all players on the server.
  • Staff may perform admin duties on their own player characters, but will not use admin powers for their own benefit while doing so.
  • Staff will not spawn items or NPCs for themselves or others to exploit the admin powers.
  • Staff will not use creative mode / fly to build their own bases.
  • Staff will not use admin powers / god mode to kill NPCs to clear dungeons or to be looted by others
  • Staff will not process admin shop / community boon exchanges for themselves or their clan members.

(5.2) ADMIN DUTIES

Please be mindful that being an admin, moderator, or helper on this server is an unpaid, volunteer gig. We do not enforce Staff "coverage", "shifts", or set any requirements for how much time they should spend doing admin duties vs playing. Ideally, we want Staff to be able to spend most of their time playing the game. Admin duties are "as-needed, as-willing". This is our leisure time as well, and we're doing our best. Please be patient and understanding 🙂
